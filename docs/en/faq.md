## I see "No data" or the app stays in loading

- Ensure you are signed in with a Google account.
- Check that your spreadsheet is in the Drive folder named `VocabQuiz`.
- Verify the selected sheet tab contains data in columns A-D.
- Confirm you have an internet connection.

![Error state](../assets/img/feature-errors.png)

## The app cannot find my Drive folder

The app looks for a Drive folder named exactly `VocabQuiz` in your Drive root. Ensure the spelling and location are correct.

## I do not see any language pairs

Language pairs are based on columns A and B. Use the supported language names listed in Settings.

## Sign-in fails

Tap **Try again** on the sign-in screen. If it continues to fail, open Settings and use **Re-authenticate** to refresh permissions.

![Sign-in screen](../assets/img/feature-signin.png)

## Google Translate does not load

The translate view is a built-in WebView. Check your network connection and try again. Some corporate networks may block translate.google.com.

## Offline use

The app requires internet access to load data from Google Sheets. If the device is offline, loading will fail.
